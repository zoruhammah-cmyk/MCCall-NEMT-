NEMT Service

Ambulatory & Door-to-Door Transport

For patients who can walk with assistance, McCall provides door-through-door ambulatory transport. A trained attendant offers a steady hand from your home to the vehicle and into your appointment.

  1. Home
  2. Services
  3. Ambulatory & Door-to-Door Transport

Who qualifies?

  • Patients who walk with a cane, walker, or human assistance
  • Elderly patients needing help to outpatient appointments
  • Post-procedure patients with temporary mobility needs
  • Patients without wheelchair or stretcher requirements

What to expect

  1. 1Attendant meets you at your door at the scheduled time
  2. 2Assisted walking to the vehicle and into your destination
  3. 3Optional wait-and-return service during your appointment
  4. 4Familiar drivers for recurring therapy or clinic visits
