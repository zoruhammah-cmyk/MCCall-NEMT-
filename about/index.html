About McCall

Neighbors helping neighbors get where they need to go

From Fields Corner to every corner of Greater Boston — we've built our reputation one ride at a time.

Rooted in Dorchester, trusted across Boston

McCall Ambulance Service was built on a simple belief: every patient deserves dignified, reliable transportation — whether they're heading to dialysis for the hundredth time or coming home from surgery.

From our headquarters on Gibson Street in Fields Corner, we've grown into one of Greater Boston's most dependable NEMT providers. Our crews live in the communities we serve. They know the hospitals, the back roads, and the families who count on us.

We're not a national franchise or a ride-share app with a medical sticker. We're your neighbors — licensed, insured, and committed to showing up on time, every time.

Background-checked crews

Every driver and attendant passes CORI/SORI background checks, driving record review, and reference verification before their first shift.

CPR, AED & first aid certified

All field staff maintain current CPR/AED certification and receive annual emergency response refresher training.

Defensive driving training

Crews complete certified defensive driving courses and quarterly road safety evaluations — critical for patient comfort and on-time arrivals.

ADA & passenger assistance

Specialized training in wheelchair securement, hydraulic lift operation, stretcher transfers, and bariatric patient handling per ADA guidelines.

Vehicle sanitization

Every vehicle is disinfected between trips using EPA-approved medical-grade cleaners. High-touch surfaces, stretchers, and lifts are sanitized after each patient.

On-time performance tracking

GPS-tracked fleet with real-time dispatch monitoring. We maintain a 94% on-time pickup rate across recurring dialysis and discharge transports.
