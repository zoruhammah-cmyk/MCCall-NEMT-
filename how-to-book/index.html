Booking Guide

How to book your NEMT ride

Three ways to schedule: call dispatch, submit our online form, or have your care coordinator book for you.

4-step booking process

1

Request a ride

Call dispatch, submit our online form, or have your care coordinator book on your behalf. Same-day and advance scheduling available.

2

We confirm details

Our team verifies pickup location, mobility needs, insurance or MassHealth coverage, and any special equipment requirements.

3

Safe transport

A certified EMT or trained attendant arrives on time, assists door-to-door, and ensures a comfortable ride to your destination.

4

Return & follow-up

Need a return trip? We schedule it before you leave. Recurring patients get priority booking and familiar faces every visit.

What to have ready

  • Patient full name and contact phone number
  • Pickup address (including apartment/unit and stairs)
  • Destination facility name and address
  • Appointment date and time
  • Mobility level (ambulatory, wheelchair, or stretcher)
  • Insurance or MassHealth member ID
  • Whether a return trip is needed
  • Special needs (oxygen, bariatric, language preference)
